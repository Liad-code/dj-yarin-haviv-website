Your Options
Option A: WhatsApp Business API (Complex)
What it is: Official API that lets you send messages programmatically.
Requirements:

WhatsApp Business Account (not regular WhatsApp)
Meta Business verification
Approved message templates
Third-party provider (Twilio, MessageBird, 360dialog) — costs $0.05-0.15 per message
24-hour messaging window rules

Verdict: Overkill for a DJ landing page. Takes days to set up, has ongoing costs.

Option B: SMS Instead of WhatsApp (Simpler)
Send an SMS to Yarin when someone submits the form.
How it works:

User submits form
Server sends SMS to Yarin's phone with the lead details
User sees a "thank you" message on the page
Yarin gets the SMS and can reply or call

Providers:

Twilio — ~$0.05 per SMS
Vonage — similar pricing
Local Israeli providers (cheaper for IL numbers)

Verdict: Simpler than WhatsApp API, reliable, fast to implement.

Option C: Email Notification (Simplest)
Send an email to Yarin when someone submits the form.
How it works:

User submits form
Server sends email to Yarin with lead details
User sees a "thank you" message
Yarin checks email and contacts the lead

Providers:

Resend — free tier (100 emails/day)
SendGrid — free tier (100 emails/day)
Nodemailer with Gmail — free but less reliable

Verdict: Easiest to implement, zero cost, good enough for most cases.

Option D: Keep WhatsApp Redirect (Current)
The redirect approach is actually the industry standard for WhatsApp lead capture. It:

Guarantees the message is sent (user presses send)
Gets the user into a WhatsApp conversation with Yarin
Works on all devices without API setup

Verdict: What you have now is the most reliable WhatsApp approach.

My Recommendation
For immediate launch: Keep the WhatsApp redirect (Option D). It works, it's free, and it puts the user directly in conversation with Yarin.
If Yarin insists on no redirect: Go with Option C (Email) as a quick addition. Yarin gets notified, then he can reach out to the lead via WhatsApp himself.
Best of both worlds: Do both — redirect to WhatsApp AND send an email notification as backup. If the user doesn't complete the WhatsApp message, Yarin still has their details.
