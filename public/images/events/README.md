# Event Type Background Images

This folder contains background images for the event types section on the homepage.

## Required Images

Replace the placeholder images with elegant, mature photos for each event type:

### 1. `wedding.jpg`
**Event Type:** חתונות (Weddings)
**Suggested Image:** 
- Elegant wedding reception with soft lighting
- Dancing couples at a wedding
- Sophisticated wedding decor with flowers and candles
- Style: Romantic, elegant, warm tones (rose, pink, purple)

### 2. `birthday.jpg`
**Event Type:** ימי הולדת (Birthdays)
**Suggested Image:**
- Stylish birthday celebration with modern decor
- People dancing at a birthday party
- Elegant birthday setup with balloons and lights
- Style: Festive, vibrant, warm colors (amber, orange, red)

### 3. `corporate.jpg`
**Event Type:** אירועי חברה (Corporate Events)
**Suggested Image:**
- Professional corporate event or gala
- Business networking event with elegant setting
- Corporate party with sophisticated crowd
- Style: Professional, modern, cool tones (blue, indigo, slate)

### 4. `bnei-mitzvah.jpg`
**Event Type:** בר/בת מצווה (Bar/Bat Mitzvah)
**Suggested Image:**
- Energetic bar/bat mitzvah celebration
- Young people dancing at a party
- Colorful, fun party atmosphere
- Style: Youthful, energetic, bright colors (cyan, teal, emerald)

### 5. `private.jpg`
**Event Type:** מסיבות פרטיות (Private Parties)
**Suggested Image:**
- Intimate private party with friends
- Exclusive nightclub or lounge atmosphere
- Private celebration with DJ setup
- Style: Exclusive, vibrant, rich colors (violet, purple, fuchsia)

## Image Specifications

- **Format:** JPG or WebP
- **Dimensions:** Minimum 800x600px (landscape orientation)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 4:3 or 16:9
- **Quality:** High quality, well-lit photos
- **Subject:** Focus on the atmosphere and people enjoying the event
- **Mood:** Professional, elegant, mature (avoid overly casual or amateur-looking photos)

## Tips for Selecting Images

1. Use photos that show people enjoying themselves but remain tasteful
2. Ensure good lighting that highlights the DJ/music aspect
3. Avoid overly dark or unclear photos
4. Consider the color overlay that will be applied (see component code)
5. Photos with depth work well with the zoom effect on hover

## Sources

Consider using:
- Unsplash (unsplash.com) - Free high-quality event photography
- Pexels (pexels.com) - Free stock photos of parties and events
- Your own professional event photography from past gigs
